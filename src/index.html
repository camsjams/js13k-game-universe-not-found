Universe Not Found 

||------ Captains Log ------||

After a failed deep space mission, a small fleet finds itself adrift in a universe unknown.

They must travel through black holes looking for a Goldilocks Universe.

No one knows what happens upon entering a black hole. The fleet has rigged their ships to leave markers behind to provide a trail of their doomed journey in the event of a failed mission.

Thankfully, each ships is equipped with a C1 level research probe piloted with AI Techs.

Minimize casualties, and know that not everyone will survive.

P.S. Avoid unknown ships - we most focus on survival.
